About Me

I am a software engineer with over 8 years of hands-on programming experience, including 4.5+ years in the industry within a dynamic product-based company. Passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems, I use code as a tool to create software that significantly improves people’s lives. Beyond my professional expertise in navigating the complete software development lifecycle, I extend my passion for coding to address challenges encountered in my daily life.

What sets me apart is not only my technical proficiency but also my innovative approach. I bring a unique blend of adaptability and creativity to every project, whether collaborating seamlessly in a team or working independently with self-motivation. Committed to continuous growth and staying abreast of the latest industry trends, I ensure that my contributions align with cutting-edge practices in software engineering while utilizing code to address real-world challenges beyond the professional realm.

I completed my Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Calicut, India in 2017. From 2017 till the end of 2021, I worked as a software engineer at Target India’s Bangalore office, working on various projects in the Supply Chain and Transportation domain. I left Target to pursue my Master’s in Software Systems Engineering at RWTH Aachen University starting in Winter 2021. During my Master’s, I kept myself engaged by working as a student assistant, first at the University itself and later at Fraunhofer FIT. I have since completed my Master’s and am currently looking for opportunities in the software engineering domain.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new technologies and building personal projects. My other hobbies include watching Arsenal and Formula 1, playing badminton and travelling with my friends.
